17. Hermanto membeli 2 karung gula pasir Dengan total beratnya mencapai 100 kg dan tara 2%. Jika harga 1 kg gula pasir adalah Rp. 8.500 per kg. Berapa rupiah yang harus dibayar Hermanto? * 5 poin Rp. 850.000 Rp. 855.000 Rp. 800.000 Rp. 833.000


Jawab:

Rp. 833.000

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah

Berat 1 karung = 100/2 = 50 kg

Berat bersih (netto) = (100%-2%) x 50 = [tex]\frac{98}{100}[/tex] x 50 = 49 kg

Berat bersih 2 karung = 2 x 49 kg = 98 kg

Harga yang harus dibayarkan = 98 x 8500 = Rp. 833.000 (D)

Tahun 3 : 30.250.000 * 10% = 3.025.000

Tahun 4 : 33.275.000 * 10% = 3.375.000

Tahun 6 : 40.315.000 * 10% = 4.031.500
                40.315.000 + 4.031.500 =44.346.500
Tahun 7 : 44.346.000 * 10% = 4.434.600
                44.346.000 + 4.434.600 = 48.781.100

23. Berdasarkan Tabel Neraca PT Maju Jaya per 31 Desember 2019 di bawah ini, hitunglah rasio likuiditas (current ratio dan quick ratio), solvabilitas (total asset to debt ratio dan net worth to debt ratio).


° AKUNTANSI °

Diketahui

Kewajiban lancar = 160.606Aktiva lancar = 672.099Total utang = 431.606Modal sendiri = 359.493Total aset = 841.099

Ditanya

current ratio = ?quick ratio = ?total asset to debt ratio = ?net worth to debt ratio = ?

Jawab

1. Current Ratio

[tex] Current \: ratio = \frac {aktiva\:lancar}{kewajiban\:lancar} \\ Current\:ratio = \frac {672.099}{160.606} \\ Current\:ratio = 4,18 [/tex]

2. Quick Ratio

[tex]Quick \:ratio = \frac {Aktiva\:lancar - persediaan}{kewajiban \:lancar} \\ Quick\: ratio = \frac {672.099 -270.000}{160.606} \\ Quick\:ratio = 2,50[/tex]

3. Total asset to debt ratio

[tex] Total\:asset\:to\:debt\:ratio = \frac {Total \: asset}{total \:debt} \\ Total\:asset\:to\:debt\:ratio = \frac {841.099}{431.606} \times 100\%\\ Total\:asset\:to\:debt\:ratio = 1,95\% [/tex]

4. Net worth to debt ratio

[tex] Net\:worth\:to\:debt\:ratio = \frac {modal\:sendiri}{total\: hutang} \times 100\% \\ Net\:worth\:to\:debt\:ratio = \frac {359.493}{431.606} \times 100\% \\ Net\:worth\:to\:debt\:ratio = 0,83\% [/tex]
