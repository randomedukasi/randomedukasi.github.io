Before we go with the answers, let us re-visit the meaning of Antonyms. Antonym is a word that has the opposite meaning to another.

Below are the antonyms of the question asked (the antonyms are after symbol ><, and related to personality traits):

1. Diligent >< idle, inactive, lazy
2. Generous >< cheap, stingy, inconsiderate
3. Friendly >< aloof, hostile, unfriendly
4. Easy-going >< uptight, agitated, rough 
5. Creative >< uncreative, unimaginative, inept
6. Two-faced >< genuine, honest, frank
7. Rebellious >< obedient, devoted, loyal
8. Aloof >< friendly, cordial, sociable
9. Reliable >< unreliable, undependable, untrustworthy
10. Untidy >< tidy, neat, orderly, organized
11. Intelligent >< brainless, dull, unintelligent
12. Patient >< complaining, fed-up, impatient
13. Stingy >< charitable, generous, freehanded
14. Emotional >< emotionless, impassive
15. Smart >< clumsy, dull, stupid
