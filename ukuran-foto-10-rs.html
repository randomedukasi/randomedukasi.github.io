A. x = 7

B. PQ = 24 cm

C. RS = 24 cm

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

PQ = 4x - 4

RS = 2x + 10

PQ = RS

4x - 4 = 2x + 10

4x - 2x = 10 + 4

2x = 14

x = 7

PQ = 4x - 4 = 4.7 - 4 = 28 - 4 = 24 cm

RS = 2x + 10 = 2.7 + 10 = 14 + 10 = 24 cm
