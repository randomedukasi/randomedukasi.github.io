1. bahasa inggris selamat hari guru


In English, the statement or expression of 'selamat hari guru' is the same of any other ones, like for the birthday atau the anniversary and so on. The proper translation of it is 'happy teacher's day'.

Penjelasan:

In translating the statement or expression about certain national day in Indonesia to English language, one needs to consider the essential part of translation work such as

appropriate vocabularycontext

The literal or word-by-word translation is not recommended as it could confuse the native speaker and the speaker of the target language. Such can be seen from the example below

'selamat hari guru' in literal translation becomes 'congratulation day of the teacher'. In the proper translation, it becomes 'happy teacher day'

Do you see the difference? The first one is potentially confusing while the second one is not at all. It is similar as saying 'happy anniversary' or 'Happy Ied Mubarak'.
