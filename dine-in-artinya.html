12. Me and family...(dine) at restaurant now


• PRESENT CONTINUOUS TENSE •

✐ formula : S + tobe + V1-ing

Me and family...(dine) at restaurant now
→ Me and family «are dining» at restaurant now

• menggunakan present continuous karena ada time signal 'now', yg berarti mereka sedang makan sekarang
• menggunakan tobe 'are' karena subjectnya plural atau > 1 orang (me & family)
