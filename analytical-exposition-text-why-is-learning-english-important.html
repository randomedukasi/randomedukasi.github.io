Jawaban:         Why is Learning English Important?

Language is the mean of communication. Mostly groups of society have their own languages. Some of them appear the top global languages. Most people in the world hear about English, Arabic, and Mandarin. However English is the most global spoken language. Then if a man wants to catch a global goal, he has to master English.

Everyone recognize that English is an international language. English is used in writing and speaking by many people all over the world. It can be either as a first or second language. We even hear British, American, Australian, and even Singaporean English. Those various names of English are used as the first language in those countries. Furthermore, some countries have their own languages as a mother language but also use English mostly in daily communication.

Besides usage in daily interaction, English is also used as a key to open doors leading to scientific and technical knowledge. No wonder we find manual guides and instructions of many devices written in English. Even if we have a pack of instant noodle, we will see the cooking instruction also written in English. This transfer of science and technology will include many countries in economic, social and politics development.

Finally, the most easily seen in the importance of learning English is that most top requirement in filling job opportunities is the ability using English; active or passive. Job applicants who master English are more favorable than ones who do not.

The above facts prove that everybody needs to learn English if he likes to greet the global era.

4. what is the future of explanation text, procedure text, analytical exposition dan hortatory exposition?​


Explanatory text is a text that explains the process of occurrence or formation of a natural phenomenon or social event. In explanatory text, an event occurs because there is an incident another before, and that event will result in another event after that.

Procedure text is a text that presents the steps of activities or various stages to do or produce something.

Analytical Exposition is a text that tries to provide a comprehensive or thorough explanation of a problem by carefully presenting supporting opinions.

Hortatory Exposition is a type of persuasive text or oral material, often used to explain whether something should not be done.

6. what is the purpose of the text analytical exposition? what is the purpose of the analytical exposition text?


║EXPOSITORY ║

Explanation

ANALYTICAL EXPOSITION TEXTGenre teks yang menjabarkan pandangan penulis terhadap suatu kasus tertentu yang dianggap penting untuk didiskusikan.

SOCIAL FUNCTION/ COMMUNICATIVE PURPOSETo convince the readers that there is an important thing to consider.

( untuk meyakinkan para pembaca bahwa ada hal penting yang seharusnya diperhatikan )

GENERIC STRUCTURE

1. Thesis

Bagian ini  dijabarkan sebagai awal pembukaan paragraf, menjabarkan rumusan pengenalan masalah yang didiskusikan berdasarkan cara pandang si penulis.

2. Arguments

Paragraf selanjutnya menjabarkan opini-opini si penulis  dengan berlandaskan informasi akurat berdasarkan bukti fakta guna mendukung argumentasinya.

3. Reiteration

Bagian akhir paragraf berisi kesimpulan / pengulasan kembali  tujuan dari pemikiran si penulis yang telah dipaparkan pada  bagian thesis.

LANGUAGE FEATURES { Unsus kebahasaan }Penggunaan conjunctive adverb ( konjungsi yang menjabarkan adanya hubungan sebab akibat,  perbandingan , ketidaksinambungan ( kontras )  

Contoh  : However, Additionally , So, meanwhile, consequently, as a result, and etc

Penggunaan simple present tense  

=> Karena berdasarkan fakta kebenaran, tenses keseluruhan menggunakan simple present tense.

Penggunaan passive voice ( tindakan aksi difokuskan utama )

Example : the children are exposed..........,  

Penggunaan  expression words

Contoh : in my conclusion.....,  i think that.....,

Cramming is Bad

How do you study when the test is coming? Do you start preparing for the test weeks or months before the test or leave things to the last hour? If you start studying weeks or months before the test, it is great. However, if you study all the material in the last hour or minute, it is not good for you and it is called cramming.

Cramming is the situation when students stay up until morning to study before their test. This habit can lead to negative impacts for some reason. The first reason is that disruptions in the regular sleep cycle can cause temporary intellectual lapses. For most students, less sleep can make them could not focus in the class. Additionally, cramming can leave us with “something that I have seen before” in our memory. However, being able to recognize something is not the same as being able to recall it. Besides that, each person has their different sleeping time, so some of them often use a stimulant for cramming. Furthermore, that stimulant gives bad effect to its consumers.

24. What is the comunicative purpose analytical exposition text?


Secara bahasa, “Analytical” bermakna memeriksa atau menguji sesuatu secara hati-hati. Dan “Exposition” memiliki arti penjelasan secara luas. Sehingga, secara harfiah, Analytical Exposition adalah teks yang mencoba untuk memberikan secara komprehensif atau menyeluruh tentang suatu masalah dengan menampilkan pendapat-pendapat pendukung secara hati-hati.

Language, "Analytical" means examining or testing something carefully. And "Exposition" has a broad explanatory meaning. Thus, literally, Analytical Exposition is a text that tries to provide comprehensively or comprehensively about a problem by presenting the supporters' opinions carefully
