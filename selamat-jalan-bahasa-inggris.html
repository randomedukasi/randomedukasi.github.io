One of the successful implementations of patient safety in the Inpatient Room is by implementing the role of the head of the room in carrying out five functions consisting of planning, organizing, staffing, directing and controlling.

Translations:

Background: Patient safety is a top priority in service as well as an important aspect of quality management. One of the successful implementations of patient safety in the Inpatient Room is by implementing the role of the head of the room in carrying out five functions consisting of planning, organizing, staffing, directing and controlling.

Discussion: A well-executed management function is a positive provision for the head of the room in leading and as a motivator. The better the management function carried out by the head of the room, the higher the quality of nurses in implementing patient safety in the inpatient room.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban merupakan materi pembelajaran Translations (terjemahan) dan menggunakan bentuk kalimat Simple Present Tense karena menjelaskan fakta hasil penerapan management yang mampu meningkatkan kualitas pelayanan bagi keselamatan para pasien medis dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Simple Present Tense:

Nominal: (+) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + Adjective / Noun / Adverb

Verbal: (+) Subject + Verb-1 S/ES + Object
