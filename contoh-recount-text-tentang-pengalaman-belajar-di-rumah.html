3. contoh Recount Text tentang pembelajaran dirumah​


Jawaban:

Recount text about Online School

Penjelasan:

Orientation:

Due to the Coronavirus / COVID-19 pandemic, we are now staying at home more and have switched to distance learning. Currently, it has been two weeks of learning from home.

Events:

I have tried to develop a routine while at home so that I can do my work efficiently. Usually, I would wake up at 6:30am and make my bed. After that, I would do a short workout to have my body moving. Once I finish my workout, I would take a shower and have breakfast. At around 9:30am, I would go to my desk and open my laptop to start working on the assignments sent to me by my teachers. I would stop at around noon because my mom would call us for lunch. Once I finished lunch, I would continue back to my desk and continue studying until 4:00pm.

After finishing all my assignments for the day, I would relax by watching YouTube videos until 6:00pm. At around this time, my mom would be preparing dinner so I would help her. We would serve dinner at around 7:00pm. After dinner, I would do the dishes, take a shower and brush my teeth. At night, I would continue watching YouTube videos until 11:00pm and then go to sleep.

Reorientation:

By keeping a routine that I made, it helped me stay focus with my studies while at home and have time for enjoyment. Creating a schedule has helped me adapt to studying at home.

Translation:

Orientasi:

Karena pandemi Coronavirus / COVID-19, kita sekarang lebih sering di dalam rumah dan telah beralih ke pembelajaran jarak jauh. Saat ini sudah dua minggu kami belajar dari rumah.

Peristiwa:

Saya telah mencoba membuat rutin saat di rumah supaya saya dapat melakukan pekerjaan sekolah dengan effisien. Biasanya, saya akan bangun jam 6:30 dan membereskan tempat tidur. Setelah itu, saya akan melakukan olahraga singkat agar tubuh saya bergerak. Setelah berolahraga, saya mandi dan sarapan. Sekitar jam 9:30, saya akan pergi ke meja belajar saya dan membuka laptop untuk mulai mengerjakan tugas yang dikirim oleh guru. Saya biasanya berhenti jam 12 siang karena mama saya panggil untuk makan siang. Setelah makan saing, saya lanjut kembali di meja belajar saya dan belajar sampai jam 4 sore.

Setelah menyelesaikan semua tugas saya untuk hari itu, biasanya saya bersantai sambil nonton video YouTube sampai jam 6 sore. Sekitar jam ini, mama saya menyiapkan makan malam jadi saya membantu dia. Kami biasanya menyajikan makan malam jam 7. Setelah makan malam, saya cuci piring, mandi dan sikat gigi. Di malam hari, aku melanjutkan nonton video YouTube sampai jam 11 malam lalu tidur.

Reorientasi:

Dengan menjaga rutinitas yang saya buat, itu membantu saya tetap fokus dengan studi dan punya waktu untuk beristirahat sementara di rumah. Membuat jadwal telah membantu saya beradaptasi dengan pembelajaran jarak jauh.

8. MEMBUAT TEXT RECOUNT TENTANG PENGALAMAN BELAJAR DIRUMAH SELAMA PANDEMI COVID-19"


Recount Text tentang pengalaman belajar di rumah selama pandemi COVID-19:

In March 2020, I have my first experience of online learning. Since the COVID-19 pandemic has spread widely around the nation, all the students have to do online learning from home.

On my first day, I was having some difficulties. While I was having an online class, I couldn't hear my teacher very well because she was having a connection problem. I could not understand the material so I have to learn it by myself. And also, it was boring because I can not meet my friends in school. But on the other side, it was fun because I can get more focus on learning by myself and my teachers provided us with fun activities like having quizzes in Kahoot.

It was neither a good nor a bad experience. I was getting used to it for a year, and now I am having direct learning activities in school.

Penjelasan:

Recount text merupakan salah satu jenis teks dalam Bahasa Inggris yang berisi tentang cerita suatu tindakan maupun kegiatan penulis atau tokoh-tokoh dalam cerita tersebut. Kegiatan yang dimaksud merupakan pengalaman dari penulis yang diungkapkan melalui recount text. Tujuan recount text adalah untuk menghibur pembaca serta memberikan informasi tentang pengalaman penulis.

Recount text memiliki tiga struktur utama atau generic structure sebagai berikut.

Orientation, berisi informasi mengenai tokoh, tempat kejadian, kapan kejadian berlangsung dan lain sebagainya. Informasi yang diberikan oleh penulis diharapkan dapat memberikan pengetahuan atau informasi yang diperlukan oleh pembaca agar dapat memahami jalan cerita.Events, merupakan isi dari teks atau berupa cerita mengenai kejadian maupun pengalaman yang ingin disampaikan oleh penulis kepada pembaca. Reorientation, struktur ketiga berisi mengenai kesimpulan maupun rangkuman atau pengulangan informasi yang ada pada struktur orientation.

15. Contoh cerita recount text tentang pengalaman belajar bahasa inggris


Kelas : VII
Pelajaran : bahasa Inggris
Kategori : recount text
Kata kunci : writer's experience, past tense

Pembahasan:
Recount text is a text which retells about writer's past experience. It usually uses past tense (verb2).

Example of recount text about studying English experience.
I studied English first time when I was in junior high school. (Saya belajar bahasa Inggris pertama kali ketika saya di SMP). I never knew about English before. (Saya tidak pernah tahu tentang bahasa Inggris sebelumnya) So, I was very excited to study English. (Sehingga, saya sangat tertarik untuk belajar bahasa Inggris).

But, suddenly I hated English very much because of the English teacher. (Tetapi, tiba-tiba saya sangat membenci bahasa Inggris karena guru bahasa Inggrisnya) He taught us very discipline. (Beliau mengajar kami dengan sangat disiplin). If we said one wrong word, he would ask us to repeat it again and again. (Jika kami mengucapkan satu kata yang salah, beliau akan meminta kami mengulanginya berulang kali). If we answered wrong question, he would give us another exercises. (Jika kami menjawab pertanyaan dengan salah, beliau akan memberikan kami soal-soal yang lain). I was really afraid to come to his class. (Saya sangat takut untuk datang ke kelas beliau).

But, I never gave up. (Tetapi, saya tidak pernah menyerah). I tried to study it myself. (Saya mencoba mempelajarinya sendiri). I repeated the pronounciation and did his homework carefully. (Saya mengulangi pengucapan dan mengerjakan PR-nya dengan hati-hati). And he wasn't angry anymore. (Dan beliau tidak marah lagi). He gave me compliments. (Beliau memberi saya pujian-pujian). After that, I know English is easy to study. (Setelah itu, saya tahu bahasa Inggris mudah untuk dipelajari). So, I study it seriously and happily. (Jadi, saya mempelajarinya dengan serius dan senang).

19. Contoh pengalaman belajar di rumah (recount teks​


Penjelasan:

Aulia semestinya memulai semester baru hari ini (16/3/2020) di sekolahnya yang terletak di Bandung Jawa Barat. Namun semua batal karena sekolah, lewat surat edaran, memutuskan para siswa belajar dari rumah.

Surat edaran tersebut berdasarkan keputusan Gubernur Jawa Barat Ridwan Kamil yang disampaikan Minggu. Instruksi belajar di rumah berlaku selama 15 hari, atau hingga 30 Maret 2020.

Langkah tersebut dilakukan untuk menekan angka pasien yang terpapar Corona atau COVID-19. Per Senin, ada 10 orang dinyatakan positif Corona di Jawa Barat.

Orangtua Aulia, Ahmad, mengatakan kebijakan ini membuatnya tenang. "Bisa lihat anak di rumah. Kan, orangtua mah tahunya cuma nganter sampai gerbang. Di kelas tahu-tahu ada yang batuk, kemudian sakit, gimana? Takutnya itu," kata Ahmad kepada reporter Tirto, Senin.

Ahmad sempat ketar-ketir karena di Bandung sulit mencari masker dan cairan pembersih tangan sejak awal Maret. Dua benda tersebut ia cari-cari dari lama untuk mencegah Aulia terdampak Corona seandainya tetap bersekolah.

"Saya pikir si anak enggak bisa izin, jadi sempat cari masker sama hand sanitizer. Alhamdulilah akhirnya diliburkan dulu," katanya bersyukur.

Meski meminimalisasi potensi penyebaran, belajar di rumah juga ada dampak negatifnya. Bagi Aulia, belajar di rumah--dengan memaksimalkan teknologi--tidak begitu efektif. Guru memberikan tugas secara online. Komunikasi juga jadi hanya satu arah, katanya.

Aulia bercerita kalau para guru sudah memberikan tugas berupa esai. Tugas tersebut dikirimkan dalam bentuk file Microsoft Word lewat WhatsApp. "Hari ini guru biologi kasih soal, apa arti virus, ciri-ciri virus. Nanti kalau sudah dijawab, tinggal dikirim. Ada juga soal di Google Class gitu (website) berupa tanya jawab," terang siswi kelas 1 SMA tersebut.

Setiap hari akan ada dua tugas dari dua mata belajaran. "Enggak tahu jadwal pelajarannya apa, tapi harus siap aja kalau dikasih soal."

Saban kesulitan mengisi jawaban, Aulia akan memanfaatkan Google untuk mencari informasi, yang tentu saja sulit diverifikasi guru apakah sumber yang ia pakai kredibel atau tidak.

Strategi belajar di rumah, menurut, Sekretaris Jenderal Federasi Serikat Guru Indonesia (FSGI) Heru Purnomo, sudah tepat, setidaknya dari sisi kesehatan. Namun untuk efektivitas pembelajaran, ia menilai perlu ada yang dipersiapkan sekolah dan guru guru.

27. contoh recount text pengalaman pramuka


Recount Text adalah teks yang bertujuan untuk menceritakan pengalaman atau kejadian yang dialami di masa lampau.

Generic Structure dari Recount text adalah:

Orientation: Berisi pengenalan tokoh, tempat, waktu dan tujuan cerita.Events: Berisi hal-hal yang terjadi pada waktu itu (lampau).Re-orientation: Berisi kesimpulan cerita atau kesan dari penulis/ tokoh terhadap pengalaman/ ceritanya.

Language features dari Recount Text:

Menggunakan Past tenseMenggunakan action verbmenggunakan thinking verb.
Pembahasan

Recount Text tentang pengalaman kegiatan pramuka:

This is my experience in middle school, exactly 3 years ago when I was in 7th grade. Every year, our school held scout activities which must be followed by all 7th grade students and at the end of each semester there were camp activities held at the school.

When the D-day arrived, before the activity began the opening ceremony was held to get information and direction during the camp activities. After that, my team mate and I immediately set up a tent that was assisted by a coach. After the tent was established, we immediately arranged the equipment we had brought. Shortly, the whistle rang, I quickly put on a tie and other scout gear because there would be a game between teams to train the cohesiveness and togetherness of each team. Then there was heavy rain. Considering that all the equipment was in the tent, the school finally decided that all students would take refuge in the hall, let the conditions where it was not possible to come in because there were several tents which had collapsed by the wind, and the field were very muddy. Arriving at the hall, we immediately rushed to the bathroom to change our clothes.

After each team gathered according to each class, everyone got a box of rice which could be enjoyed together. After dinner, the rain stopped. We went straight to the courtyard to enjoy the campfire and watch the performances of each team, in the form of songs and poems. After the campfire event was over, the school announced that the female students in that night must sleep in the meeting room, while the men remained in the hall. Finally we were moved again. In our hearts, we were very happy because of the meeting room has an air conditioner, and it might not be fair for men to have to sleep without air conditioner.

The next morning at around 5 a.m, all students had to get up and get ready, because morning exercise would be held. After that, we enjoyed the breakfast together. We didn't forget to clear the tent that had collapsed and saved it again. Around 8 o'clock, we prepared for the closing ceremony. This was an unforgettable experience.
