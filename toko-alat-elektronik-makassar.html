2. apa bahasa inggrisnya toko buah,toko sayuran,toko baju,toko perlengkapan alat tulis , toko bunga,toko alat elektronik,toko roti di tunggu jawabannya.. .....


Answer: 

The English words of those places are as follows:
- toko buah : fruit shop / fruit stall (for the ones on the side of the street)
- toko sayuran: greengrocer 
- toko alat tulis: stationery 
- toko bunga: florist's
- toko alat elektronik: electronic store
- toko roti: baker's / cake shop (for specialising on cakes)

In English, each shop has different term, especially the ones with specialised goods.
