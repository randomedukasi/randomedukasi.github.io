Personal Letter

Dear friend,

How are you doing? I hope this letter finds you in good health. I am writing to catch up with you and see how things have been going for you. It has been such a long time since we last talked and I have been missing our conversations.

I have been busy lately with school and my part-time job, but I always find time to think about you. I would love to hear about your life and all the things that have been keeping you busy. I am also eager to hear about your future plans and what you want to do after graduation.

I hope you will take some time to write back and let me know how you are doing. I miss our friendship and I hope that we can reconnect soon.

Take care and I hope to hear from you soon.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]

I am so glad to receive your letter and to hear from you again. I have been doing well and keeping busy with my studies and extracurricular activities. Thank you for asking!

It has been a while since we last talked, but I have been thinking about you too. I am so excited to hear about your future plans and what you want to do after graduation.

I hope that we can make plans soon to catch up and spend some time together. I have missed our friendship and I would love to strengthen our bond once again.

24. apa ciri ciri dari personal letter dan bahasa apa yang digunakan dalam personal letter?


Personal letter bersifat informal. Dapat dibuat untuk teman, saudara dekat, dan keluarga.
Bahasa yang digunakan adalah bahasa inggris informal (Informal English). Bagian dari personal / informal letter adalah Opening, Body, dan Closing.

29. personal letter adalah​


Personal letter adalah sebuah surat tidak resmi yang dikirim oleh satu individu ke individu lainnya dan biasanya berisi hal-hal pribadi. Sifat personal letter yaitu tidak resmi sehingga surat ini tidak dapat ditujukkan untuk sebuah instansi, organisasi, atau perusahaan.

Jawaban:

Letter adalah pesan tertulis atau cetak langsung atau pribadi yang ditujukan kepada seseorang atau organisasi. Jadi, personal letter adalah surat pribadi, yaitu sebuah surat informal yang dikirim dari satu individu ke individu lain yang sudah kenal baik dan isinya bersifat pribadi.
