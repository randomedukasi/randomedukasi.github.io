12. Change the words in the bracket into apprapriate verb !


Ubahlah kata-kata yang di dalam kurung ke dalam bentuk kata kerja yang tepat untuk melengkapi kalimat simple past di bawah ini!!!

1). Linda (WAS) in Lombok last Holiday.

2). How long DID she (STAY) in this hotel Last holiday?

3). I (WAS) at home yesterday.

4). Nisa (CAME) here last night.

5). It DID NOT (RAIN) this morning.

6). We (VISITED) them last week.

7). The baby (CRIED) last night.

8). My mother DID NOT (COOK) yesterday morning.

9). Mila (INVITED) her friends to the party last night.

10). DID you (MEET) her yesterday?

Rumus yang digunakan adalah :

1). Simple Past Verbal

(+) S + V2 + O

(-) S + did + not + V1 + O

(?) Did + S + V1 + O

2). Simple Past Nominal

(+) S + To be (was/were) + complement

(-) S + To be (was/were) + not + complement

(?) To be (was/were) + S + complement

Catatan!!!

* To be "was" digunakan untuk subject I/she/he/it/the third singular person.

* To be "were" digunakan untuk subject you/they/wr/plural subject
