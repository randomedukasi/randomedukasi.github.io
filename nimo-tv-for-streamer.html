▶ Cermin Cekung

f = 9 cm

s = 12 cm

s' = __ ?

jarak bayangan benda

s' = (s × f) / (s - f)

s' = (12 × 9) / (12 - 9)

s' = 108 / 3

s' = 36 cm ✔

- 1/f = 1/s + 1/s' (mencari jarak bayangan)

keterangan =

- M = s'/s = h'/h (mencari pebesaran bayangan)

keterangan =

s = jarak benda dari cermin , s' = jarak bayangan , h' = tinggi bayangan , h = tinggi benda

penyelesaian soal :

D1 :

so = 12 cm

f = 9 cm

D2 :

D3 :

1/f = 1/s + 1/s'

1/s' = 1/f - 1/s

= 1/10 - 1/12

= 6/60 - 5/60

1/s' = 1/60
