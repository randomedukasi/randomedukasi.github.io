Jawaban:

50 km/jam

Penjelasan:

Waktu tempuh kereta ekonomi

t = 400/40 = 10 jam

Waktu tempuh kereta eksekutif

t = 10 - 2 = 8 jam

Kelajuan kereta eksekutif

V = 400/8 = 50 km/jam

Jawaban:

Kereta Api Ekonomi

S = 400 KM

V = 40 KM/Jam

T = S÷V

= 400 ÷ 40 = 10 Jam

Kereta Api Exekutif

T = 10 - 2 = 8 Jam

S = 400 KM

V = S ÷ T

= 400 ÷ 8 = 50 Km/Jam
