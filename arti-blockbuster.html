4. Write the sentences in reported speech! a. 'I shot my first film last year,' the cameraman said. b. 'Craig has worked hard to become James Bond,' the producer said. c. 'Naomi's new film is going to be a blockbuster,' the critic said. d. 'I'll never act with Henry again,' the actor said.


DIRECT - INDIRECT SPEECH

a. The cameraman said (that) he had shot his first film the previous year.

b. The producer said (that) Craig had worked hard to become James Bond

c. The critic said (that) Naomi's film was going to be a blockbuster

d. The actor said (that) he would never act with Henry again

EXPLANATION

Direct speech merupakan pernyataan yang disampaikan langsung oleh narasumber. Direct speech terpapar dalam bentuk percakapan dengan disertai tanda petik (" )

Sedangkan, Reported speech (Indirect speech), merupakan pemberitaan ulang pernyataan yg disampaikan oleh narasumber kepada orang lain di kurun waktu berbeda dengan tanpa mengubah informasi yg tersirat.

Perubahan bentuk noun clause verb pada reported speech bergantung pada bentuk reporting verb dan jenis informasi yg disampaikan pada direct speech, apakah berupa informasi faktual, imperative, interrogative dan prohibition.

Jika reporting verb berbentuk lampau { said, told, asked }, noun clause verb mengalami backshift :

↪Simple present➡Simple past

↪Simple past➡Past perfect

↪Present perfect➡Past perfect

↪Simple future ➡Present conditional

↪Present continuous➡Past continuous

↪Past continuous➡Past perfect continuous

Ini dikecualikan jika pernyataan yang disampaikan merupakan informasi faktual, tidak mengalami backshift meskipun reporting verb-nya bentuk lampau.

Example :

1⃣

D.S : Catalina said, "I am learning the reported speech now."

I.S : Catalina said (that) she was learning the reported speech at that time.

2⃣

D.S : Gilbert said, "The sky is blue."

I.S : Gilbert said (that) the sky is blue.

Keterangan :

Contoh pernyataan pertama mengalami backshift pada noun clause, diantaranya :

1. mulai dari verb tense yg awalnya menggunakan present continuous menjadi past continuous

2. Pronoun (kata ganti orang), siapa yang mengucapkan pernyataan tersebut. Awalnya menggunakan "I" berubah menjadi "she" didasarkan pada gender dari nara sumber

3. Keterangan waktu mengalami perubahan, yg awalnya "Now" menjadi "at that time" didasarkan pada situasinya.

Sedangkan, contoh pernyataan kedua, tidak mengalami perubahan verb tense yg digunakan, meskipun reporting verb-nya adalah bentukan lampau "said." Ini didasarkan pada informasi yg disampaikan adalah fakta, fenomena alam.
