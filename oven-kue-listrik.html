P= 2200 W
t = 8 H = 8X3600s = 28800s
v = 220v
ditanya
a R?
b W?
jawab
P= I²R
V = IR
P = V×I
2200W = 220v x I
I = 10 A
a R = V ÷ I
   R = 220v ÷ 10A
   R = 22 ohm
b W = P x t
   W = 2200 × 28800
