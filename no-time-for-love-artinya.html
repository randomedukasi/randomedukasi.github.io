In fact, he managed to establish a school of the National University Student Park (National Institute of Taman Siswa Onderwijs) on July 3rd 1922. At first the Dutch colonial government attempted to deter his plan. Dutch government issued a Wild School Ordinance on October 1st 1932. Finally, he was declared as Father of National Education of Indonesia, as well as his birth day serves as National Education Day. Ki Hajar Dewantara died on 26th April 1959 in Yogyakarta.
